## Scoring Rubric

Each prospect is scored 0-3 on five dimensions (max 15):
- **ICP fit:** high-stakes technical workflow, not generic chatbot
- **Pain evidence:** visible reliability, eval, governance, or human-review problem
- **Urgency:** governance-specific deadline, active rollout, recent reliability/eval post, regulatory pressure, or production-risk signal
- **Reachability:** `3` = warm intro/direct email/active GitHub or known DM route; `2` = plausible public route but no confirmed send target; `1` = findable only
- **Detrix fit:** gates, audit, evaluator feedback, or domain-specific acceptance criteria would clearly help

Priority bands:
- **Send today:** score >= 13 and revenue or high-conversion peer target with plausible route
- **Send Mon-Tue:** strong score but weaker route, lower near-term urgency, or better fit as a softer design-partner ask
- **Nurture / enrich:** score <= 10, weak reachability, academic credibility lead, or contact details missing

Trust boundary: this matrix uses public web, GitHub/API, official pages, search-engine-indexed snippets, and human-reviewable URLs only. LinkedIn profile pages were not visited or scraped. LinkedIn snippet leads require manual review before outreach.
